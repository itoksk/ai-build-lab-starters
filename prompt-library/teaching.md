# Classroom & teaching prompts

Prompts for classroom AI builds — tutors, idea-hearings, AI apps, and demo openers. Verbatim from the `/prompts` page.

> Live: https://ai-build-lab.pages.dev/prompts

## A tutor Gem for a weak subject

```
I want a tutor Gem focused on a subject I'm weak at. It explains at my level and gives hints a little at a time.
```

## Team idea-hearing (don't decide for us)

```
We are a team. Our SUKI are: ___, ___, ___. Be our sounding board and interview us to find one hackathon idea. Ask one question at a time. Don't decide the idea for us.
```

```
We are a team. Our SUKI x AI is a tool that turns the songs we love into practice quizzes.
```

## AI apps that use a camera / sensors

```
Use MediaPipe to build an app that analyzes a basketball shooting form. Compare it to a correct form and show what to improve.
```

```
Use Teachable Machine to classify trash from a camera photo and tell me how to sort it under our local rules.
```

## Stop it coding too soon (during a hearing)

```
Stop. Don't write code. Ask me one question at a time first.
```

## Demo opener (one-liner)

```
Our SUKI x AI is a tool that turns the songs we love into practice quizzes.
```

## Letter to future me

```
Write a short letter to me, one year from today. Remind me what I built this week, what I was proud of, and one promise I want to keep. Use my voice.
```
