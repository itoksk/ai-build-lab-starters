# Role / audience / task / constraint prompts

The difference between weak and strong prompts: **Role, Audience, Task, Constraint**. Add these four. Prompts are verbatim from the `/prompts` page.

> Live: https://ai-build-lab.pages.dev/prompts

| Strength | Prompt |
|---|---|
| Weak | `What are the best things about Kobe?` |
| Medium | `List 3 great things about Kobe, for a middle school student, no emojis.` |
| Strong | `You are a travel guide. An exchange student just arrived in Kobe. Plan their perfect first Saturday, with one spot only locals know.` |

The weak one drops all four levers and stays generic; the strong one carries all four and becomes a tailored plan.

## Role prompting

Open with a role and the whole conversation shifts. Specific roles beat vague ones.

```
あなたは○○の専門家です
```

(You are an expert in ___. Set it first; "a middle-school math teacher" beats "an expert".)
